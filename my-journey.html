From aspiring scientist to people leader.

If you had asked 10-year-old Kalpana what she wanted to be, the answer would have been simple: a scientist, an astronaut, or a doctor. An HR professional? Not even close. Growing up in a Hindi-speaking household, I became determined to master English. Books became my teachers, and movies became my classroom. I spent countless hours reading, listening, and repeating pronunciations until they felt natural.

Looking back, I didn't realize it then, but I was already building one of the skills that would shape my future: the ability to learn, adapt, and grow in unfamiliar situations. My academic journey began in Biochemistry with dreams of working in healthcare and research. But somewhere along the way, I realized that while I enjoyed understanding systems, I was even more fascinated by understanding people.

That realization changed everything. What started as an unexpected career shift eventually became a profession I genuinely love.

2018
Zashed Fashiontech
Scaled workforce from 7 to 30 employees in 2 months
Building Without a Blueprint

My first full-time role was with a fast-moving fashion startup. Within two months, we scaled rapidly from 7 employees to 30 employees.

With no established frameworks or inherited systems, I built hiring processes, onboarding pathways, and foundational engagement initiatives completely from the ground up.

What it taught me

Growth needs structure.

Culture doesn't happen by accident. It must be intentionally built.

2020
OSIM India
Finding My Feet in a Crisis

Following a swift leadership transition, I unexpectedly stepped up to independently manage the corporate HR function right as COVID hit.

As an essential healthcare service organization, we remained operational, navigating health uncertainties, complex regulations, and daily challenges with complete transparency.

What it taught me

Leadership isn't always about solving every problem instantly.

Sometimes it's about staying present, listening intentionally, and building a sense of stability when your teams need it most.

Discovering the Power of Data

My tenure at boodmo scaled my approach by infusing people analytics into workplace culture. I began exploring how logical datasets reinforce human experiences.

By evaluating talent assessments, hiring metrics, and workforce indicators, we translated data-driven logic directly into continuous organizational growth.

What it taught me

Data doesn't replace empathy—it strengthens it.

The more objectively we evaluate people trends, the more sound, inclusive, and supportive our long-term decisions become.

The Scalers
Building Happiness as Infrastructure

Today, I work with high-growth companies to help international tech teams feel truly supported and at home. My focus is on creating simple, human-centered processes that help managers do their best work while ensuring our culture stays strong as we grow.
